Overview

Australian Migration Statistics, 2024–25 — Released November 2025.

Produced as an accompaniment to Australia's Migration Trends. Detailed tables for the financial year including 10-year comparative data. Coverage: permanent and temporary migration, citizenship, Net Overseas Migration and labour market.

Period: 2024–25 financial year, published annually.

Caveats

For statistical and research purposes. Cite the Department as source. Views do not necessarily represent the Department of Home Affairs. Commonwealth excludes liability for use or reliance on this information.

Contents

Permanent migration (1.0–1.18), temporary visas (2.0–2.4), Humanitarian (3.0–3.4), visa non-compliance (4.0–4.4), NOM (5.0–5.1), citizenship (6.0), labour market (7.0–7.8). More at data.gov.au.

Data Items & Terminology

Applicant Type
Primary vs secondary applicants; 'primary' excludes dependants.
Citizenship
Country of citizenship; where multiple, travel document or nominated.
Client Location
Onshore (in Australia) or offshore at time of application.
Humanitarian Program
Offshore resettlement and onshore protection components.
Migration Program
Three streams: Skill, Family, Special Eligibility.
Net Overseas Migration (NOM)
Net gain/loss via arrivals and departures (12+ months stay).
Outcome
Visas granted net of certain cancellations and exclusions.
Primary / Secondary Applicant
Principal vs dependant under Migration Regulations.
